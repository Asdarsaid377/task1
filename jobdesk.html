Senior Product Designer

location_on Remote
schedule Full-time
payments $120k - $160k

Job Description

We are looking for a passionate Senior Product Designer to join our team. You will be responsible for creating beautiful and intuitive user experiences for our core platform, shaping the future of how people find and manage their professional opportunities.

As a key member of our design team, you'll collaborate closely with engineers, product managers, and stakeholders to translate complex requirements into elegant, simple solutions. We value designers who are systems-thinkers and have a keen eye for visual craft.

Key Responsibilities

  • Lead design initiatives from discovery through to final hand-off.
  • Create high-fidelity prototypes and design specifications.
  • Maintain and contribute to our global design system.
  • Conduct user research and usability testing to validate design decisions.
